Capture full-page screenshots with free cropping and PDF export — no subscription required.

FullSnap scrolls through any webpage, captures each section, and stitches them into a single seamless screenshot. Then edit, crop, and export — all for free.

FEATURES:
• Full page capture — works on any webpage, handles lazy-loaded content
• Crop tool — select exactly the area you need with draggable handles
• Save as PNG — full resolution, one click
• Save as PDF — automatically paginated for A4 printing
• Copy to clipboard — paste into Google Docs, Slack, email, or anywhere
• Zoom controls — fit to screen or zoom into details
• Clean, dark editor UI

100% FREE:
No premium tier. No subscription. No feature gates. Everything is included.

PRIVACY FIRST:
FullSnap processes everything locally in your browser. No data is ever collected, transmitted, or stored on any server. Your screenshots never leave your computer.

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED:
• activeTab — to capture the current tab's visible content
• scripting — to scroll the page during capture
• storage — to temporarily hold screenshot segments (cleared after stitching)

Open source: https://github.com/nelagueye/fullsnap
