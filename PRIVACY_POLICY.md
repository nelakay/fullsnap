# Privacy Policy for FullSnap

**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

## Summary

FullSnap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. Period.

## Data Collection

FullSnap collects **no data** of any kind. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is tracked
- No analytics or telemetry is sent
- No cookies are used
- No data is transmitted to any server

## How It Works

All processing happens entirely within your browser:

- Screenshots are captured using Chrome's built-in tab capture API
- Image stitching and editing happen locally in your browser
- Exported files (PNG, PDF) are saved directly to your device
- No data ever leaves your computer

## Permissions

FullSnap requests the following browser permissions:

- **activeTab**: To capture a screenshot of the current tab
- **scripting**: To scroll the page during capture
- **storage**: To temporarily hold screenshot data between capture and editing (cleared immediately after use)
- **unlimitedStorage**: To handle large full-page screenshots

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/nelagueye/fullsnap).

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted to this page and the GitHub repository.
